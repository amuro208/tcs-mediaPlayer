    2. Period 2 will start at 2pm on 24/09/15 and ends at 10pm on 26/09/15 (Promotional Period 2)

    5. HOW TO ENTER

  5. To enter the promotion, Entrants must achieve 3 out of 3 successful kicks in the NRL Golden Boot kicking experience during the Promotional Period.

  8. All eligible Entries received during each Promotional Period will be automatically stored electronically and the winners will be selected at random by a computer.
